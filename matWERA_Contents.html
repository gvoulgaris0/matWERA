Matlab functions (matWERA) for reading the binary files created by the WERA science package

This is a collection of MATLAB functions used to read the binary files collected by the WERA HF Radar systems developed by Klaus-Werner Gurgel at the University of Hamburg and commercially available by Helzel Messtechnic GmbH
